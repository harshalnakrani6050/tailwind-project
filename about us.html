Welcome To

Mayuresh Super Specialty Hospital is situated in Navi Mumbai, a twin city of Mumbai.

The only one goal of the Hospital is to see happy & satisfied faces of their patients

The Hospital is Centrally Air-conditioned and maintains utmost hygiene, ensuring safety and quality service to the patients. The team at the Hospital is friendly and caring, the Consultants, Staff Nurses, Aaya, Maushi, Paramedics all strive hard to fulfill the requirement of the patients and arrange all facilities under one roof. All the staff is well trained and experienced each performing its duty to the best of their abilities and training provided at the Hospital. Special mentioned of the Doctors who are committed to their duties and ensures proper guidance, procedures and best treatment to the patients.
