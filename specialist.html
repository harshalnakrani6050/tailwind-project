Role Of Internal Medicine In Vashi, Navi Mumbai

Internal medicine (general internal medicine) is the medical specialty dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases in adults. The specialty manages patients with multiple simultaneous conditions or complex comorbidities.

Mayuresh Hospital, Navi Mumbai Internal Medicine Department offers comprehensive care for patients with a wide range of health needs including complex and serious ailments. The department has expertise in taking primary care of adult patients and plays a key role in managing all aspects of their physical, emotional, and psychological wellbeing including prevention and early detection of diseases. The centre utilises a collaborative approach to provide specialised, effective treatment by evaluating medical risks before and after surgery, enhancing continuity of care, and facilitating communication between patient and rest of the clinical team. They work closely with experts across medical and surgical specialties such as cardiovascular medicine, endocrinology, gastroenterology, medical oncology, nephrology, orthopaedics, pulmonary medicine, and rheumatology.

Internal Medicine in Vashi, Navi Mumbai at Mayuresh Hospital

Internal Medicine in Vashi, Navi Mumbai at Mayuresh Hospital plays a crucial role in providing comprehensive healthcare services. Internal Medicine focuses on diagnosing, managing, and treating a wide range of adult health conditions and diseases.

At Mayuresh Hospital, the Internal Medicine department is responsible for:

  • Diagnosis and Treatment:

    • Internal Medicine specialists are skilled in diagnosing complex medical problems. They manage various conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, respiratory issues, infectious diseases, gastrointestinal problems, and more.

  • Preventive Care:

    • They emphasize preventive measures and health screenings to detect diseases early, aiming to prevent their progression and complications.

  • Holistic Patient Care:

    • Internal Medicine physicians focus on the overall health of the patient, considering various factors that contribute to well-being, including lifestyle, mental health, and chronic disease management.

  • Collaborative Approach:

    • They often work in collaboration with other specialists, coordinating care for patients with multiple health issues or complex medical conditions.

  • Continuity of Care:

    • Internal Medicine doctors provide ongoing care, managing chronic conditions, adjusting treatments, and monitoring the patient's progress to ensure the best possible outcomes.
